About This App

Meghalaya Exam Preparation is a comprehensive platform designed to help candidates prepare for various government exams in Meghalaya, including MPSC LDA cum Typist and other competitive examinations.

This platform offers two modes:

  • Practice Mode: Validate answers immediately and view solutions
  • Mock Test Mode: Simulate real exam conditions with timers

Features

  • Multiple exam categories and question papers
  • Detailed solutions
  • Video explanations via YouTube
  • Track your progress
  • Guest mode for quick practice
  • Test history and analytics

Security and Privacy

Your data is secure. For production deployment, encrypt your question bank before publishing.
