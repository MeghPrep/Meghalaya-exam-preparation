Meghalaya Exam Preparation Platform

About This App

Meghalaya Exam Preparation is a comprehensive platform designed to help candidates prepare for various government exams in Meghalaya, including MPSC LDA cum Typist and other competitive examinations.

This platform offers two modes of practice:

  • Practice Mode: Validate answers immediately and view detailed solutions
  • Mock Test Mode: Simulate real exam conditions with customizable timers

Features

  • Multiple exam categories and question papers
  • Detailed solutions for every question
  • Video explanations via YouTube integration
  • Track your progress with user accounts
  • Guest mode for quick practice
  • Comprehensive test history and analytics

Security & Privacy

Your data is secure. For production deployment, ensure to encrypt your question bank before publishing to protect intellectual property.
